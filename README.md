# Playful Numeracy

An AI-enabled project exploring scalable approaches to foundational numeracy assessment, competency mapping, and remediation within FLN frameworks.

## Introduction

India's Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) mission aims to ensure that every child acquires essential reading, writing, and numeracy skills during the early years of schooling. Research has consistently shown that when foundational skills are not acquired early, learning gaps widen over time and become increasingly difficult to address.

Frameworks such as NIPUN Bharat define learning outcomes, competencies, and developmental milestones that guide foundational learning. However, translating these frameworks into scalable classroom resources, assessments, and learning support systems remains a significant challenge.

## Current Scope

As an initial step, this project focuses on numeracy assessment generation for students in Classes 2–4.

The current MVP explores a pipeline that can:

* Extract concepts from educational content
* Map concepts to FLN competencies and learning outcomes
* Align content with established frameworks
* Generate assessment questions and worksheets
* Support competency-based remediation pathways

This assessment-focused pipeline serves as a foundation for broader FLN-aligned learning systems in the future.

## For Whom

* Students in Classes 2–4 (current project scope)

## Objective

To develop a system that can:

* Identify concepts from educational content
* Map concepts to FLN competencies and learning outcomes
* Align content with NIPUN Bharat, ASER, and state-board curricula
* Generate assessments and worksheets
* Explore scalable approaches to competency-based learning and remediation

## Idea

### Competency-Driven Design

Educational content is analyzed through the lens of concepts, competencies, and learning outcomes rather than chapter boundaries alone.

### Structured Learning Progression

Concepts are organized across developmental learning levels to support competency-based assessment and remediation.

### Content-to-Assessment Pipeline

Educational materials can be transformed into structured assessment resources through extraction, concept identification, competency mapping, and question generation workflows.

### Framework Alignment

Generated resources are designed to remain grounded in established FLN frameworks and learning outcomes.

### Human-Centred AI

AI serves as an enabling layer for educators, curriculum designers, and researchers rather than replacing educational expertise.
