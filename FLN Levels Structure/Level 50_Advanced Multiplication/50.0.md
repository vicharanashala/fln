# **Main Level 50.0**

### **Description**

Students independently solve complex multiplication problems involving two-digit by two-digit and three-digit by one-digit multiplication. 

They apply place value concepts and the standard multiplication algorithm to solve mixed multiplication problems accurately.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Two-Digit × Two-Digit**

**Examples**

24 × 13  
36 × 21  
48 × 15

### **Section B: Three-Digit × One-Digit**

**Examples**

324 × 4  
518 × 6  
742 × 3

### **Section C: Missing Number**

**Examples**

24 × □ \= 288  
□ × 6 \= 432
