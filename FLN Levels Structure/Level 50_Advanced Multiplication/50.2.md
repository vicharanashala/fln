# **Sub-Level 50.2**

### **Description**

Students strengthen multiplication skills through guided two-digit multiplication and simple three-digit multiplication. 

Activities emphasize place value alignment and step-by-step calculation.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Two-Digit × One-Digit**

**Examples**

34 × 3  
42 × 2

### **Section B: Three-Digit × One-Digit**

**Examples**

120 × 2  
204 × 3

### **Section C: Fill the Missing Number**

Simple multiplication facts.
