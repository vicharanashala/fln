# **Sub-Level 50.1**

### **Description**

Students solve multiplication problems using smaller two-digit numbers and simpler three-digit by one-digit multiplication while reinforcing the standard multiplication algorithm.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Two-Digit × Two-Digit**

**Examples**

12 × 14  
23 × 11

### **Section B: Three-Digit × One-Digit**

**Examples**

215 × 3  
341 × 2

### **Section C: Complete the Multiplication**

**Examples**

18 × □ \= 180
