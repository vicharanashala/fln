# Sub-Level 26.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support while reinforcing carry addition. 

Students solve simple two-digit plus one-digit problems where carrying occurs in an obvious and structured manner. 

Visual aids and place-value representations may be used.

* ### Solve straightforward carry-addition problems
