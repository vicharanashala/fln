# Sub-Level 9.1

### **Description**

This sub-level provides structured practice and evaluation for basic pattern recognition and line/shape tracing.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Pattern Recognition**

* Identify and complete simple repeating visual patterns (A-B-A-B style).

#### **Section B: Draw by Tracing**

* Trace dotted lines to connect objects.
* Trace simple pictures and numbers.
