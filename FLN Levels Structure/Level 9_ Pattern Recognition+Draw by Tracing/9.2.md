# Sub-Level 9.2

### **Description**

This sub-level provides structured activities for shape-based pattern continuation and tracing numbered geometrical shapes.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Pattern Recognition**

* Complete geometric patterns using simple shapes (e.g., star, circle).

#### **Section B: Draw by Tracing**

* Trace geometric shapes (circle, square, triangle) in numerical order to complete the drawing.

### **Examples**

* Complete the pattern: ⭐ ● ⭐ ● ⭐ __
* Shape Tracing: Trace numbers 1-2-3-4 to form a square.
