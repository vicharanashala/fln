# **Sub-Level 56.2 (Easiest)**

### **Description**

Students develop an understanding of area and perimeter through counting, measuring, and simple visual activities. They recognize that perimeter measures the boundary while area measures the space inside a shape.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Count the sides to find the perimeter.

(Simple rectangles and squares)

---

### **Section B**

Count shaded squares to find the area.

---

### **Section C**

Match

Shape ↔ Area

Shape ↔ Perimeter

---

### **Section D**

Circle the shape with the larger area.
