# Main Level 22.0

### **Description**

This is the main assessment level for Level 22.0. Students demonstrate mastery of target concepts before progressing.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Ascending Order**

Arrange numbers from smallest to largest.

* Ascending:
* 23, 18, 30, 15
* Answer: 15, 18, 23, 30

#### **Section B: Descending Order**

Arrange numbers from largest to smallest.

* Descending:
* 23, 18, 30, 15
* Answer: 30, 23, 18, 15
