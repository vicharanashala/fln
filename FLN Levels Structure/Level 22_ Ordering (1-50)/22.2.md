# Sub-Level 22.2

### **Description**

This sub-level provides further remediation for students who continue to struggle with ordering numbers.
Activities include visual supports such as number lines, object groups, and clearly distinguishable quantities to help children understand the concept of arranging numbers in order.

### **Examples**

* Using a number line:
* 10   11   12   13   14   15
* Arrange:
* 15, 11, 13
* Answer: 11, 13, 15
