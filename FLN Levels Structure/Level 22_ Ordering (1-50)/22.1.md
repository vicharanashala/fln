# Sub-Level 22.1

### **Description**

This sub-level contains questions that are easier than the main level.
The activities use fewer numbers and larger gaps between values, making it easier for children to identify the correct sequence.

### **Examples**

* Ascending:
* 12, 18, 25
* Answer: 12, 18, 25

* Descending:
* 12, 38, 15
* Answer: 18, 15, 12
