# Sub-Level 32.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support using highly visual positional activities. 

Students identify positions through movement, ordering, and real-life visual contexts.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Stairs Position**

* Identify who is standing on a specific step.

#### **Section B: Race Position**

* Identify who finished in a given position.

#### **Section C: Queue Position**

* Identify positions in a line.

#### **Section D: Picture-Based Practice**
