Sub-Level: 12.1

### **Description**

* This sub-level contains easier questions with strong visual support.   
* Students work primarily with pictures, bundles, and grouped objects to understand the relationship between tens and ones.   
* The focus is on recognizing groups of ten and counting remaining objects.
