Sub-Level: 12.2

### **Description**

* This sub-level provides additional support for students who are still developing an understanding of place value.   
* Activities focus on building the idea of a group of ten through counting and grouping exercises.   
* Students work with simple visual examples and small quantities to strengthen foundational understanding.
