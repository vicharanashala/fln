# **Main Level 49.0**

### **Description**

Students independently apply place value concepts to read, write, and represent four-digit numbers. 

They build numbers from place values, convert between standard and expanded forms, and solve number sequence activities involving four-digit numbers.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Place Value**

Identify the place value of each digit.

**Examples**

4728

* Thousands \= \_\_\_  
* Hundreds \= \_\_\_  
* Tens \= \_\_\_  
* Ones \= \_\_\_

9054

* Thousands \= \_\_\_  
* Hundreds \= \_\_\_  
* Tens \= \_\_\_  
* Ones \= \_\_\_

### **Section B: Standard & Expanded Form**

Convert between standard and expanded forms.

**Examples**

4528 \= 4000 \+ 500 \+ 20 \+ 8

3000 \+ 700 \+ 40 \+ 2 \= \_\_\_\_\_\_

### **Section C: Build the Number**

Form the number using place values.

**Examples**

* 6 Thousands \+ 2 Hundreds \+ 5 Tens \+ 9 Ones  
* 8 Thousands \+ 0 Hundreds \+ 4 Tens \+ 7 Ones

### **Section D: Missing Numbers**

**Examples**

2998 \_\_ 3000

4899 \_\_ \_\_ 4902

6995 \_\_ 6997

### **Section E: Number Sequences**

**Examples**

2450, 2451, 2452, \_\_\_, \_\_\_

3600, 3700, 3800, \_\_\_

5400, 5300, 5200, \_\_\_
