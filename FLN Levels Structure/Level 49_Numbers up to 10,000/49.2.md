# **Sub-Level 49.2**

### **Description**

Students strengthen their understanding of four-digit numbers through guided place value activities, number formation, and simple counting patterns. Visual place value models support the transition from three-digit to four-digit numbers.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Count the Blocks**

Use base-ten models to identify the number.

### **Section B: Match**

Match the place value representation with the correct number.

### **Section C: Expanded Form**

Examples

1000 \+ 300 \+ 40 \+ 5 \= \_\_\_\_\_\_

4000 \+ 200 \+ 6 \= \_\_\_\_\_\_

### **Section D: Complete the Sequence**

Examples

1000, 1001, \_\_\_

2000, 2100, 2200, \_\_\_
