# Main Level 28.0

### **Description**

This level evaluates mastery of comparison concepts using numbers up to 100\. 

Students compare numbers independently, and solve mixed comparison problems involving greater than, less than, and equal to relationships.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Symbol Selection**

* Choose the correct symbol (\> , \< , \=).

#### **Section B: Greater and Smaller Number**

* Identify the greater number.  
* Identify the smaller number.

#### **Section C: Missing Number Comparison**

* Complete comparison statements.

### **Examples**

* 58 \_\_ 61  
* 72 \_\_ 72  
* Which is greater: 84 or 79?  
* \_\_ \> 65  
* 43 \_\_ 38
