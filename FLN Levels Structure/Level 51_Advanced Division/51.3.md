# **Sub-Level 51.3**

### **Description**

Students reinforce division through simple two-digit division problems and equal grouping activities. 

The focus is on building fluency in exact division before progressing to larger numbers.

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Two-Digit ÷ One-Digit

Examples

24 ÷ 2  
36 ÷ 3  
48 ÷ 4

### **Section B**

Picture-Based Equal Groups

Examples

Divide **24 apples** equally into **4 baskets**.

Write how many apples are in each basket.

### **Section C**

Division Facts

Examples

54 ÷ 6  
42 ÷ 7  
35 ÷ 5
