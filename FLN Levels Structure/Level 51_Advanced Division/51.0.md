# **Main Level 51.0**

### **Description**

Students independently solve long division problems involving two-digit and three-digit numbers. 

They apply division with and without remainders and use division to solve real-life mathematical situations.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Three-Digit ÷ One-Digit**

**Examples**

864 ÷ 4  
936 ÷ 6  
735 ÷ 5

### **Section B: Division with Remainders**

**Examples**

745 ÷ 6  
527 ÷ 4

### **Section C: Missing Number**

**Examples**

□ ÷ 8 \= 12  
648 ÷ □ \= 81
