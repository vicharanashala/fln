# **Sub-Level 51.1**

### **Description**

Students solve three-digit division problems using the long division algorithm. 

Problems involve both exact division and simple remainders while reinforcing place value understanding.

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Three-Digit ÷ One-Digit

Examples

624 ÷ 3  
525 ÷ 5

### **Section B**

Division with Remainders

Examples

458 ÷ 3  
635 ÷ 4

### **Section C**

Missing Number

Examples

□ ÷ 5 \= 26

### **Section D: Picture Division**

Example

4 baskets

↓

248 oranges

↓
