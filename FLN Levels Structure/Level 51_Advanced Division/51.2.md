**Sub-Level 51.2**

### **Description**

Students strengthen division skills through exact three-digit and two-digit division problems. 

Activities emphasize correct long division steps and place value.

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Three-Digit ÷ One-Digit

Examples

420 ÷ 2  
360 ÷ 4

### **Section B**

Two-Digit ÷ One-Digit

Examples

84 ÷ 4  
72 ÷ 6

### **Section C**

Complete the Division

Examples

96 ÷ □ \= 12
