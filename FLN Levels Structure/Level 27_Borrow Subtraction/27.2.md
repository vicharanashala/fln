# Sub-Level 27.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support while reinforcing borrow subtraction. Students solve simple two-digit minus one-digit problems where borrowing is required in an obvious and structured manner. Visual aids and place-value representations may be used.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Guided Borrow Subtraction**

* Solve straightforward borrow-subtraction problems.

#### **Section B: Picture-Based Problems**

* Use visual representations to subtract quantities.
