# Sub-Level 28.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support while covering all comparison concepts. 

Students compare clearly distinguishable numbers and use visual aids, number lines, or objects to understand greater than, less than, and equal to relationships.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Basic Symbol Selection**

* Choose the correct comparison symbol.

#### **Section B: Greater Number**

* Circle the bigger number.

#### **Section C: Smaller Number**

* Circle the smaller number.

#### **Section D: Equal Numbers**

* Identify equal pairs.

#### **Section E: Picture-Based Comparison**

* Compare quantities using visual representations.

### **Examples**

* 15 \_\_ 29  
* 48 \_\_ 48  
* Circle the greater number: 62, 35  
* Circle the smaller number: 21, 45
