# Sub-Level 34.2 (Easiest)

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support through visual comparison activities. Students identify differences in length, weight, and capacity using pictures without reading measurements.

### **Sections**

### **Section A: Length**

Two pencils

Circle the longer one.

### **Section B: Weight**

Balloon

Basketball

Circle the heavier one.

### **Section C: Capacity**

Cup

Bucket

Circle the one that holds more.
