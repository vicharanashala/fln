# Main Level 14.0

### **Description**

This is the main assessment level for Level 14.0. Students demonstrate mastery of target concepts before progressing.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Matching (Object ↔ Number)**

* The child needs to count the no of objects given and match it with the correct numeral
* Number range: 1-30

#### **Section B: Fun – Draw by Tracing**

* Trace dotted lines to complete fun drawings by following numbers from 1 to 30.

### **Examples**

* Match objects: (Group of 15 apples) ↔ 15
* Draw by Tracing: Trace a line connecting numbers 1 through 30 to draw a house.
