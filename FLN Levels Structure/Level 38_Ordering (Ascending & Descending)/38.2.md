# Sub-Level 38.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support using simple ordering activities with clearly distinguishable three-digit numbers. Students reinforce ordering concepts through structured practice.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Ascending Order**

**Examples**

* 100, 300, 200  
* 450, 650, 550

### **Section B: Descending Order**

**Examples**

* 800, 600, 700  
* 900, 400, 500

### **Section C: Smallest & Largest**

**Examples**

* 150, 250, 350  
* 910, 810, 710
