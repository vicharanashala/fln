# Sub-Level 38.1

### **Description**

This level covers the same ordering concepts using fewer numbers and simpler place value comparisons. 

Students arrange numbers with greater visual clarity and reduced complexity.

## 

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Ascending Order**

**Examples**

* 245, 198, 321  
* 504, 450, 540

### **Section B: Descending Order**

**Examples**

* 620, 705, 680  
* 900, 850, 875

### **Section C: Smallest & Largest**

**Examples**

* 412, 321, 289  
* 740, 704, 470
