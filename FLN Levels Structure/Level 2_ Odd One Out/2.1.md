# Sub-Level 2.1

### **Description**

This sub-level covers the "odd one out" concept with slightly reduced difficulty. The category differences are more distinct, making it easier for students to identify the mismatch.

Identify the mismatched item in groups of 3-4 objects.

### **Examples**

* Identify the odd one out: [Car, Bus, Train, Flower] (The odd one out is Flower, as the others are vehicles).
* Identify the odd one out: [Red Circle, Blue Circle, Green Circle, Red Square] (The odd one out is Red Square, as the others are circles).
