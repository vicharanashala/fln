# Main Level 2.0

### **Description**

This level evaluates the student's ability to identify the "odd one out" from a group of objects. Students analyze a set of items and select the one that does not belong based on category, shape, size, color, or function.

Identify the object that does not belong in a group of 4-5 items.

### **Examples**

* Identify the odd one out: [Apple, Banana, Orange, Cat] (The odd one out is Cat, as the others are fruits).
* Identify the odd one out: [Square, Circle, Triangle, Table] (The odd one out is Table, as the others are geometric shapes).
