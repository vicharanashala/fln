# **Sub-Level 54.1**

### **Description**

Students practice fraction addition and subtraction with smaller denominators using visual support. 

Activities reinforce combining and separating equal parts through pictures.

### **Sections**

## **Section A: Fraction Addition**

**Examples**

* 1/4 \+ 2/4 \= \_\_\_  
* 2/3 \+ 1/3 \= \_\_\_  
* 1/5 \+ 3/5 \= \_\_\_

## **Section B: Fraction Subtraction**

**Examples**

* 3/4 − 1/4 \= \_\_\_  
* 3/5 − 2/5 \= \_\_\_  
* 5/6 − 1/6 \= \_\_\_

## **Section C: Match**

Match the shaded picture with the correct answer.

## **Section D: Colour the Answer**

Colour the remaining or combined fraction.

**Examples**

* 1/4 \+ 2/4  
* 2/5 \+ 1/5  
* 4/6 − 2/6
