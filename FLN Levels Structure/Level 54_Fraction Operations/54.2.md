# **Sub-Level 54.2**

### **Description**

Students develop fluency in fraction operations through highly visual activities. 

They combine and remove equal parts using fraction models before solving symbolic expressions.

### **Sections**

## **Section A: Add the Shaded Parts**

Observe the pictures and write the resulting fraction.

## **Section B: Remove the Shaded Parts**

Observe the pictures and write the remaining fraction.

## **Section C: Match**

Match the picture with the correct fraction operation.

## **Section D: Complete the Whole**

Complete the picture by shading the missing part.

**Examples**

* 3/4 is shaded → Shade the remaining 1/4.  
* 2/5 is shaded → Shade the remaining 3/5.  
* 5/8 is shaded → Shade the remaining 3/8.
