# Sub-Level 29.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support while covering all ordering concepts. 

Students arrange small sets of clearly distinguishable numbers and use visual aids, number lines, or objects to understand number order.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Basic Ascending Order**

* Arrange 3 numbers from smallest to largest.

#### **Section B: Basic Descending Order**

* Arrange 3 numbers from largest to smallest.

#### **Section C: Smallest and Largest Number**

* Identify the biggest and smallest number.

#### **Section D: Picture-Based Ordering**

* Order quantities represented through objects or pictures.

### **Examples**

* Arrange in ascending order: 12, 29, 18  
* Arrange in descending order: 45, 21, 37  
* Which is the largest? 34, 52, 28  
* Which is the smallest? 61, 17, 42
