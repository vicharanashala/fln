# Sub-Level 29.1

### **Description**

This level covers the same ordering concepts with reduced complexity. 

Students arrange fewer numbers and work with sets that have more obvious differences, making sequencing easier while still reinforcing ascending and descending order.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Ascending Order**

* Arrange 3–4 numbers from smallest to largest.

#### **Section B: Descending Order**

* Arrange 3–4 numbers from largest to smallest.

#### **Section C: Smallest and Largest Number**

* Identify extreme values before ordering.

### **Examples**

* Arrange in ascending order: 25, 41, 18  
* Arrange in descending order: 62, 35, 49  
* Smallest number: 37, 19, 42
