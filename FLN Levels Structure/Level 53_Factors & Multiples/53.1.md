# **Sub-Level 53.1**

### **Description**

Students practice identifying factors and multiples of smaller numbers while strengthening multiplication relationships.

Activities focus on listing factors, writing multiples, and recognizing simple common factors.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Factors**

Examples

* Factors of **12**  
* Factors of **15**  
* Factors of **20**

### **Section B: Multiples**

Examples

Write the first four multiples of:

* 5  
* 8  
* 10

### **Section C: Match**

Match the number with its factors.
