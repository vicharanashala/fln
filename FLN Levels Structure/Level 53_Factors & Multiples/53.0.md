# **Main Level 53.0**

### **Description**

Students independently identify factors, multiples, factor pairs, common factors, and common multiples using multiplication and division concepts.

Activities encourage logical reasoning and number sense.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Factors**

Find all the factors.

**Examples**

* Factors of **24**  
* Factors of **36**  
* Factors of **18**

### **Section B: Multiples**

Write the first five multiples.

**Examples**

* Multiples of **7**  
* Multiples of **9**  
* Multiples of **12**

### **Section C: Common Factors & Common Multiples**

**Examples**

* Common factors of **12** and **18**  
* Common multiples of **4** and **6**
