**Main Level: 1.0**
This is the main level that every student has to clear, and then only he/she will be promoted to the next level.
This level will contain 2 sections: SECTION A - Equal Quantity 
				            SECTION B - More/Less

### SECTION A: EQUAL
This will contain 2 columns, and the child has to match the equal things.




### SECTION B: MORE/LESS
In this section, the questions will be given in pairs, and the child has to circle the one with the greater value.
