# Sub-Level 1.1

### **Description**

This sub-level covers basic quantity comparison with slightly reduced complexity compared to the main level. Students focus on matching equal groups of objects and determining more or less using small numbers.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Equal Quantity**

* Match objects across two columns that have the same quantity (numbers 1 to 5).

#### **Section B: More/Less**

* Compare two groups of objects and circle the group that has more or less.

### **Examples**

* Match equal objects: (Group of 3 apples) ↔ (Group of 3 oranges)
* Circle the group with more items: (4 stars) vs (2 stars)
