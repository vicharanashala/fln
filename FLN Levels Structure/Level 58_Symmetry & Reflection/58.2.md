# **Sub-Level 58.2 (Easiest)**

### **Description**

Students develop an understanding of symmetry through highly visual activities. They recognize mirror images and identify symmetrical objects without requiring formal geometric reasoning.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Circle the picture that looks the same on both sides.

---

### **Section B**

Match each picture with its mirror image.

---

### **Section C**

Complete the missing half by tracing or drawing.

---

### **Section D**

Circle all symmetrical everyday objects.

Examples

* Butterfly  
* Flower  
* Leaf  
* Bottle  
* Ball
