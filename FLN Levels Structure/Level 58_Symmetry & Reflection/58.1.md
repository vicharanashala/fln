# **Sub-Level 58.1**

### **Description**

Students identify symmetrical shapes and complete simple mirror-image activities. They strengthen visual reasoning by recognizing lines of symmetry in familiar objects.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Circle all symmetrical shapes.

---

### **Section B**

Draw one line of symmetry.

Examples

* Square  
* Circle  
* Rectangle

---

### **Section C**

Complete the missing half.

(Simple grid-based drawings.)

---

### **Section D**

Match each object with its mirror image.
