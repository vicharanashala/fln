# **Main Level 58.0**

### **Description**

Students independently identify lines of symmetry, complete symmetrical figures, recognize mirror images, and apply symmetry concepts to geometric shapes and everyday objects.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Draw the Line of Symmetry**

Draw the correct line(s) of symmetry.

**Examples**

* Square  
* Rectangle  
* Circle  
* Equilateral Triangle

---

### **Section B: Complete the Picture**

One half of the figure is given.

Draw the missing half.

**Examples**

* Butterfly  
* Leaf  
* Heart  
* Simple geometric pattern

---

### **Section C: Mirror Image**

Choose the correct mirror image.

**Examples**

* Simple shapes  
* Letters or symbols (if appropriate)  
* Everyday objects

---

### **Section D: Identify Symmetry**

Circle the symmetrical figures.

Examples

* Star  
* Heart  
* Kite  
* Irregular shape

---
