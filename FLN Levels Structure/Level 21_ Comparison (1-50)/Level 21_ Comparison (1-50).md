# Level 21: Comparison (1-50)

## Objective

Compare numbers and identify greater than, less than, and equal quantities.

## Description

students compare two numbers and determine whether one number is greater than, less than, or equal to the other.

Children use their understanding of number values to identify relationships between numbers and apply the symbols >, <, and = correctly.

## Learning Outcome

Students can compare two numbers and determine their relationship.
