### **Main Level 52.0**

### **Description**

Students independently interpret maps containing multiple landmarks, identify locations using cardinal directions, follow multi-step routes, and determine the shortest path between two locations.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Identify Directions**

Observe the map and answer the questions.

**Example Activities**

* Which place is north of the school?  
* Which place is east of the hospital?  
* Which place is west of the park?  
* Which place is south of the library?

### **Section B: Follow the Route**

Observe the map and determine the final destination.

**Example Activities**

* Start from the house → Move North → Move East → Move East → Move South. Where do you reach?  
* Start from the school → Move West → Move South → Move East. Where do you reach?

### **Section C: Shortest Path**

Observe the map and identify the shortest route.

**Example Activities**

* Find the shortest path from the house to the playground.  
* Find the shortest path from the hospital to the market.

### **Section D: Mixed Map Interpretation**

* Locate landmarks.  
* Identify directions.  
* Follow routes.  
* Determine positions between landmarks.
