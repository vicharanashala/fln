# **Sub-Level 52.1**

### **Description**

Students practice reading simple maps with fewer landmarks while identifying directions and following short routes using cardinal directions.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Identify Directions**

Example Activities

* Which place is north of the tree?  
* Which place is east of the house?

### **Section B: Route Following**

Example Activities

* Start at the house. Move North, then East. Where do you reach?  
* Start at the park. Move South. Where do you reach?

### **Section C: Locate Landmarks**

Example Activities

* Circle the place west of the school.  
* Circle the place south of the hospital.

### **Section D: Match Directions**

Match direction arrows with North, South, East, and West.
