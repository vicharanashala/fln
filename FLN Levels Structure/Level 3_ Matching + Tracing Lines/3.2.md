# Sub-Level 3.2

### **Description**

This sub-level provides hands-on practice for matching relationships (opposites, shapes, relations) and drawing lines through basic line tracing activities.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Matching**

* Match opposites (e.g., hot and cold, big and small).
* Match identical shapes or objects of the same shape.
* Match related objects (e.g., bat and ball, key and lock).

#### **Section B: Tracing Lines**

* Trace basic dotted lines to connect related items across columns.

### **Examples**

* Match opposites: Day ↔ Night
* Match relations: Bat ↔ Ball
* Trace dotted lines to help the bee reach the flower: [Bee] - - - - - - [Flower]
