# Main Level 3.0

### **Description**

This is the main assessment level for Level 3.0. Students demonstrate mastery of target concepts before progressing.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: MATCHING**

* Children match words or pictures.

* Activities
* Opposite Matching
* Shape Matching
* Relation Matching
* Quantity Matching

 Match the opposite:
* Tall → ?
* A. Short
* B. Ball
* C. Circle
* D. Dog

 Match the shape:
* Ball → ?
* A. Triangle
* B. Circle
* C. Square
* D. Star

 Match the relation:
* Dog → ?
* A. Nest
* B. Water
* C. Puppy
* D. Cat

 Mixed
* Fish → ?
* A. Tree
* B. Water
* C. Sky
* D. Ball

#### **Section B**

* draw:
* Horizontal lines
* Vertical lines
* Slanting lines
* Semi Circle
* Curve

* This level will not contain trace lines. It will contain only 2 dots; the child has to draw in, and no traces will be provided, only ref dots.
* Col1 | col2
* Real-life objects will be shown | 2 dots which the child will connect and draw the shape of that object
