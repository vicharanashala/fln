# Sub-Level 30.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support by using direct matching between numbers and tally marks. Students focus on recognizing tally groups of five and connecting them to numerical quantities.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Match Number and Tally**

* Match written numerals directly to their corresponding tally mark groups (up to 15).

#### **Section B: Read Simple Tallies**

* Count pre-drawn tally marks (focusing on groups of 5) and write the corresponding number.

#### **Section D: More or Less**

* Compare two different tally groups and circle the one representing a larger or smaller quantity.
