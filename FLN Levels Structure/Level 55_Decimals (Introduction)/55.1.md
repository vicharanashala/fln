

# **Sub-Level 55.1**

### **Description**

Students practice reading and representing simple decimals using tenths and hundredths. Activities reinforce decimal place value through visual models and money.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Identify tenths and hundredths.

Examples

2.4

5.8

1.25

---

### **Section B**

Match decimal numbers with place value charts.

---

### **Section C**

Picture Models

Show a 10×10 grid with shaded parts.

Write the decimal.

---

### **Section D**

Money

Examples

₹8.50

₹12.25
