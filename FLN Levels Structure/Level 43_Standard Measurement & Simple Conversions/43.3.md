# Sub-Level 43.3 

### **Description**

Students develop familiarity with standard measurement units through recognition and matching activities. They identify measuring tools and associate common objects with the appropriate unit.

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Match the Tool**

📏 → Length

⚖️ → Weight

🥛 → Capacity

*(Shown with pictures rather than words where possible.)*

### **Section B: Match Object to Unit**

Examples

✏️ → cm

🏠 → m

🍎 → g

🪣 → L
