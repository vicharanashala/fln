*** Main Level: 23.0 ***

This level evaluates the student's understanding of all concepts learned from Levels 12–20.

 The assessment focuses on concept mastery rather than memorization and helps identify strengths and areas requiring additional support. 

The questions will cover:
Numbers 11–30
Numbers 31–50
Skip Counting (Frog Jump Patterns)
Number Comparison
Ordering Numbers (Ascending & Descending)
Students will be required to apply multiple skills learned in previous levels and demonstrate their overall understanding of foundational numeracy concepts.
Sections

Section A: Number Recognition & Sequencing
Missing numbers
Number identification
Number writing

Section B: Skip Counting & Patterns
Frog jump by 2s
Frog jump by 3s
Complete number patterns
Section C: Number Comparison
Greater than (>)
Less than (<)
Equal to (=)
Section D: Ordering Numbers
Ascending order
Descending order
