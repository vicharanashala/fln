# Sub-Level 33.2

### **Description**

This level provides maximum support through visual grouping activities. 

Students count objects in equal groups and determine totals using simple multiplication facts from the 2 and 3 tables.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Count Objects**

* Count objects arranged in small equal groups.

**Examples**

* 🍌🍌 | 🍌🍌  
* ⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐

#### **Section B: Count Groups**

* Identify the total quantity represented by groups.

**Examples**

* 2 groups of 2 \= \_\_\_  
* 3 groups of 2 \= \_\_\_  
* 2 groups of 3 \= \_\_\_

  #### **Section C: Complete the Pattern**

* Complete simple equal-group patterns using pictures and repeated addition.

**Example**

| Equal Groups | Repeated Addition | Total |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| 🍎🍎 | 2 | 2 |
| 🍎🍎 🍎🍎 | 2 \+ 2 | 4 |
| 🍎🍎 🍎🍎 🍎🍎 | \_\_\_ | 6 |
