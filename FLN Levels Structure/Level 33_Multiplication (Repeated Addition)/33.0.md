# Main Level 33.0

### **Description**

This level assesses mastery of multiplication concepts through equal groups, repeated addition, and multiplication facts. 

Students interpret visual groups, identify repeated addition, and complete multiplication patterns to strengthen their understanding of multiplication as repeated addition.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Count Equal Groups**

* Count total objects arranged in equal groups.  
* Determine the total quantity represented by groups.

**Examples**

* 🍎🍎🍎 | 🍎🍎🍎 | 🍎🍎🍎  
* 🐟🐟🐟🐟 | 🐟🐟🐟🐟

#### **Section B: Repeated Addition**

* Find totals using repeated addition.

**Examples**

* 3 \+ 3 \+ 3 \= \_\_\_  
* 4 \+ 4 \+ 4 \= \_\_\_

  #### **Section C: Complete the Multiplication Table**

* Complete multiplication patterns by relating equal groups, repeated addition, and multiplication expressions.

**Example**

| Repeated Addition | Multiplication | Total |
| ----- | :---- | ----- |
| 4 | 1 × 4 | 4 |
| 4 \+ 4 | 2 × 4 | 8 |
| 4 \+ 4 \+ 4 | 3 × 4 | \_\_\_ |
| \_\_\_ | 4 × 4 | 16 |
