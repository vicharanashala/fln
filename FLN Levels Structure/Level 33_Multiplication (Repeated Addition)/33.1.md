# Sub-Level 33.1

### **Description**

This level covers the same multiplication concepts with fewer groups and simpler calculations. Students use visual support and repeated addition to determine totals before applying basic multiplication facts.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Count Groups**

* Count objects arranged in equal groups.

**Examples**

* ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐  
* 🍎🍎🍎 | 🍎🍎🍎

#### **Section B: Repeated Addition**

* Add equal quantities repeatedly.

**Examples**

* 2 \+ 2 \+ 2 \= \_\_\_  
* 3 \+ 3 \= \_\_\_  
* 4 \+ 4 \= \_\_\_

  #### **Section C: Complete the Pattern**

* Complete simple multiplication patterns.

**Example**

| Repeated Addition | Multiplication | Total |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| 2 | 1 × 2 | 2 |
| \_\_\_ | 2 × 2 | 4 |
| 2 \+ 2 \+ 2 | \_\_\_ | 6 |
