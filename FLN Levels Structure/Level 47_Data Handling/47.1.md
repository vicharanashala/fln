# **Sub-Level 47.1**

### **Description**

Students interpret tally marks, pictographs, and bar graphs with fewer categories and simpler values. 

They compare quantities and answer basic questions using visual data.

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Simple tally chart.

Questions:

* Count the tally marks.  
* Circle the highest value.

### **Section B**

Simple pictograph.

**Key:** ⭐ \= 1

Questions:

* Count the pictures.  
* Which category has more?

### **Section C**

Simple bar graph.

Questions:

* Which bar is tallest?  
* Which bar is shortest?

### **Section D**

Compare two categories.
