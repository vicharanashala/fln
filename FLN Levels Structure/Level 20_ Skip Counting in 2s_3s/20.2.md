# Sub-Level: 20.2 

## Description
This sub-level is designed for students who need further remediation. 

Activities use smaller number ranges and visual supports to help students understand the concept of repeated jumps before attempting more complex patterns.

Example Activities
2, 4, __
3, 6, __

Count and jump on a number line with highlighted steps.

## Assessment Criteria
Understands the concept of increasing by a fixed amount
Completes simple skip-counting patterns with support
Shows readiness to progress to higher difficulty levels
