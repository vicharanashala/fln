*** Main-Level 20.0 ***

## Description
This is the main level where students identify, continue, and complete skip-counting patterns by 2s and 3s independently.
 
Activities include number sequences, missing numbers, and visual jump patterns. 
Students are expected to recognize the pattern and determine the next numbers without additional guidance.
Example Activities
🐸 Jump by 2
2, 4, 6, __, 10
🐸 Jump by 3
3, 6, 9, __, 15
Fill in the missing numbers:
12, 14, __, 18
