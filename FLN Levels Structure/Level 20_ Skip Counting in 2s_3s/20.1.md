# Sub-Level 20.1

## Description
This sub-level provides additional support through visual representations and guided skip-counting activities. 

Students use number lines, frog jumps, and partially completed patterns to understand how numbers increase by a fixed amount. 

The focus is on building confidence in recognizing and extending patterns.

Example Activities
🐸 Frog Jump by 2
2 → 4 → 6 → 8 → __
Number Line:
2   4   6   8   __
🐸 Frog Jump by 3
3 → 6 → 9 → __
