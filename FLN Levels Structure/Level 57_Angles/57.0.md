# **Main Level 57.0**

### **Description**

Students independently identify, classify, compare, and measure angles using visual representations and a simple protractor. They apply angle concepts to real-life situations.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A: Identify the Angle**

Identify the type of angle.

**Examples**

* Acute angle  
* Right angle  
* Obtuse angle  
* Straight angle

(Shown as diagrams.)

---

### **Section B: Measure the Angle**

Use a protractor to measure the angle.

**Examples**

* ∠ABC \= \_\_\_°  
* ∠PQR \= \_\_\_°

(Simple values such as 30°, 45°, 90°, 120°.)

---

### **Section C: Compare Angles**

Examples

* Circle the larger angle.  
* Arrange the angles from smallest to largest.
