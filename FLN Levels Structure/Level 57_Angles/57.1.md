# **Sub-Level 57.1**

### **Description**

Students classify and compare angles using clear visual diagrams. Activities focus on recognizing common angle types and making simple comparisons.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Identify:

* Acute  
* Right  
* Obtuse

---

### **Section B**

Match the angle to its name.

---

### **Section C**

Circle the larger angle.

---

### **Section D**

Find the angle in everyday objects.
