# **Sub-Level 57.2 (Easiest)**

### **Description**

Students develop a basic understanding of angles through visual observation. They recognize that angles can be smaller or larger and identify common angle types without measuring.

---

## **Sections**

### **Section A**

Circle all the right angles.

---

### **Section B**

Match identical angles.

---

### **Section C**

Which angle is bigger?

---

### **Section D**

Find angles in pictures.

Examples

* Window  
* Door  
* Clock  
* Kite
